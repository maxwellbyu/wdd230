Benin City Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

The Benin City Chamber of Commerce

The site name includes the word "Chamber" to signify the organization's purpose of representing local businesses and fostering economic development in the area.

Site Purpose

  • Provide resources and support for local businesses
  • Promote economic growth and development in the region

Scenarios

  1. What events are scheduled this month to promote business networking?
  2. Where can I find contact information for the board of directors?
  3. What is the current population growth rate in the area?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #336699 (used for headings)
  • Secondary Color: #FF9900 (used for accents)

Typography

  • Heading Font: Arial, sans-serif
  • Body Font: Georgia, serif
