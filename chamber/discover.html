About Benin City

Benin City is the capital and largest city of Edo State, southern Nigeria. It is the fourth-largest city in Nigeria according to the 2006 census, after Lagos, Kano, and Ibadan.

Information

BENCCIMA serves as a strong advocate for the local business community,and platform for networking and collaboration among businesses, entrepreneurs, and industry stakeholders.

Population and Growth

Benin City, the capital of Edo State in Nigeria, has an estimated population of approximately 1.5 to 2 million people.

Housing and Cost of Living

Benin City offers a more affordable cost of living compared to some other Nigerian cities, expenses can still vary widely depending on lifestyle choices and preferences.

Health and Safety

Benin City has various programs and initiatives aimed at improving health and safety,Currently Benin City is Safe a Safe Heaven to Live and do Business.
